Description

At Darkshade Inc., every product we make is built to last. We provide durable, easy-to-use software solutions with innovative features that customers depend on, all while protecting their privacy and data. Customers should have access to safe, reliable, and secure solutions with genuine support if they need them. Technology is transforming work in just about every sector of the global economy—and while these advances have created unparalleled opportunities for many, far too few have access to the quality education, training and job opportunities required to improve their economic and social mobility in a changing landscape.

Darkshade Inc. organization addresses the need to ensure all communities are prepared for the jobs driving the future, and to date has awarded approximately $65 million to support more than 70 organizations working to inspire, educate and prepare people of all ages to succeed in the workforce of today and tomorrow. We partner with our grantees to rethink the pathways into and through the tech sector, grow more diverse talent pipelines and fundamentally reimagine the education and workforce systems in which we operate—and while our work is far from over, we are proud of the positive impact our investments have made since the foundation was established in 2018.


We're leveraging the power of digital technology to amplify our impact and do more good in the world. Building on our longstanding investments in corporate social responsibility, we've committed to a five-year, $250 million effort to advance economic mobility, educational opportunity, health and well-being in communities around the globe. We've also launched new diversity and inclusion programs in partnership with our employee affinities—supporting African American, Latinx, LGBTQ, Pan-Asian, women and veteran communities—to create true social change.
